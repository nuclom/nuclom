| Feature | Free | Pro | Enterprise |
| ------- | ---- | --- | ---------- |
| **Response Time** | Best effort | 24 hours | 4 hours |

## Getting Support

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Documentation">
    Review our [troubleshooting guide](/guides/troubleshooting) first
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search Community">
    Check if your question has been answered in forums
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    Email support@nuclom.com with details about your issue
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to Include

When contacting support, include:

- Account email and organization name
- Description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce
- Screenshots or error messages
- Browser and device information

<Note>
  Enterprise customers should use their dedicated Slack channel for fastest response.
</Note>
