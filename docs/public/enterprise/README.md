## FAQ

**Q: What's the minimum number of users for Enterprise?**

A: There's no minimum, but Enterprise is designed for teams of 25+. For smaller teams, Pro plan may be more cost-effective.

**Q: Can we trial Enterprise before purchasing?**

A: Yes! We offer a 30-day pilot program with full Enterprise features and dedicated support.

**Q: How long does SSO setup take?**

A: Most SSO configurations are completed within 1-2 business days. Complex setups may take up to 5 days.

**Q: Is there a contract requirement?**

A: Annual contracts are standard for Enterprise. Monthly billing is available at a higher rate.

**Q: Can we migrate from Pro to Enterprise?**

A: Yes, upgrades are seamless. All your content, users, and settings are preserved.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**

A: Credit card, ACH transfer, wire transfer, and PO/invoice for annual contracts.

**Q: Do you offer non-profit or education discounts?**

A: Yes, contact enterprise@nuclom.com for special pricing.
