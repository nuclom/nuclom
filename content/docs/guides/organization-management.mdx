Organizations are shared workspaces for your team’s videos, insights, and settings.

## Organization Basics

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Organization Name" icon="tag">
    The name your team sees in the navigation and share links.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Organization Slug" icon="link">
    Appears in your URL: `nuclom.com/org/your-slug`.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  You can update the name and slug later in **Organization Settings** → **Organization**.
</Note>

## Create an Organization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the organization switcher">
    Click the organization name in the top navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Create Organization">
    Enter a name and slug for the new workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite teammates">
    Add teammates once the organization is created.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Switch Between Organizations

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the switcher">
    Click the organization name in the top navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select an organization">
    Pick another organization to switch context.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Organization Settings Overview

Use **Organization Settings** to manage the workspace:

<CardGroup cols={2}>

## Deleting an Organization

<Warning>
  Deleting an organization is permanent and removes all videos, settings, and access.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Organization Settings">
    Go to **Organization Settings** → **Organization**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete">
    Use the **Delete Organization** action in the danger zone.
  </Step>
</Steps>
