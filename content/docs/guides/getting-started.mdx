## Overview

In this guide, you will:

1. Set up your account and organization
2. Upload or import your first video
3. Explore the Knowledge Hub and sharing tools

## Step 1: Set Up Your Workspace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [nuclom.com](https://nuclom.com) with email, GitHub, or Google.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create an organization">
    Pick a name and slug for your workspace. You can update them later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite teammates (optional)">
    Visit **Organization Settings** â†’ **Members** to invite your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Add Your First Video

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Upload from your device">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Upload">
        Click **Upload Video** in the left navigation.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select a file">
        Drag and drop or browse to pick your video.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add details">
        Add a title, description, and optional channel or series.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Publish">
        Upload and let Nuclom process the content.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Import a recording">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Import">
        Click **Import Videos** from the upload area.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose a source">
        Select Google Drive, Zoom, or Google Meet.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm">
        Pick recordings to import and confirm the upload.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  Nuclom automatically generates transcripts, summaries, chapters, and action items during processing.
</Note>

## Step 3: Collaborate and Share

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your video">
    Go to **Videos** and select your upload.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share">
    Click **Share** to generate a secure link with access controls.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Comment with context">
    Add time-stamped comments in the transcript or player timeline.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 4: Explore the Knowledge Hub

The Knowledge section helps you connect insights across your organization.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Ask" icon="message-circle">
    Ask questions and get answers grounded in your video library.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Insights" icon="lightbulb">
    Review key decisions, topics, and gaps across recordings.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Graph" icon="diagram-project">
    Visualize relationships between topics, people, and content.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sources" icon="database">
    Track connected sources and imported recordings.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Navigation Basics

### Sidebar Sections

| Section | What you'll find |
| ------- | ---------------- |
| **Home** | Organization overview and recent activity |
| **Search** | Search across videos, transcripts, and topics |
| **Videos** | Full video library |
| **Collections** | Curated groupings of videos |
| **Knowledge** | Feed, Ask, Sources, Topics, Decisions, Gaps, Insights, Graph |
| **Library** | My Videos, Watch Later, History, Shared with Me, Trash |

### Command Bar

Press **Cmd/Ctrl + K** to quickly navigate, create content, or jump to settings.

## Essential Shortcuts

| Shortcut | Action |
| -------- | ------ |
| `Cmd/Ctrl + K` | Open command bar |
| `Space` | Play/pause video |
| `Arrow Left/Right` | Seek 10 seconds |
| `F` | Toggle fullscreen |
