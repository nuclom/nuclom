## Share a Video

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the video">
    Navigate to **Videos** and select the file you want to share.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Share">
    Use the **Share** button to open sharing options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set access level">
    Choose **View**, **Comment**, or **Download** and set optional expiration or password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the link">
    Copy the link and share it with internal or external collaborators.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  For internal sharing, send the direct video URL so teammates can see org-only context and insights.
</Tip>

## Leave Time-Stamped Feedback

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Comment on the timeline">
    1. Play the video to the right moment.
    2. Click the comment icon in the player.
    3. Add your note and mention teammates with @.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Comment in the transcript">
    1. Open the **Transcript** tab.
    2. Hover a segment and click the comment button.
    3. Add your feedback with precise context.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
