This guide walks through the key settings for individuals and organizations.

## Personal Settings

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Profile" icon="user">
    Update your name, avatar, and timezone.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Linked Accounts" icon="link">
    Connect GitHub or Google for faster sign-in.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Account & Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Change email or password">
    Go to **Settings** → **Account** or **Security** to update credentials.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Enable two-factor authentication">
    Use **Settings** → **Security** to add a TOTP authenticator and backup codes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Passkeys and sessions">
    Add passkeys and review active sessions from **Settings** → **Security**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Notifications

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email">
    Configure comment, mention, and video update notifications.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="In-app">
    Control alerts and quiet hours in **Settings** → **Notifications**.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Organization Settings

### Core Configuration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Organization profile">
    Update name and slug in **Organization Settings** → **Organization**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Members and roles">
    Invite teammates and manage permissions in **Members** and **Roles**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Integrations & Automations

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Integrations" icon="plug">
    Connect Zoom, Google Meet, Slack, Teams, and Zapier.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Content Sources" icon="cloud">
    Manage imported sources and sync behavior.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Security & Compliance

<Steps>
  <Step title="SSO">
    Configure SAML or OIDC under **Organization Settings** → **SSO**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Audit logs">
    Review access and admin actions in **Audit Logs**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Developer Access

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="API Keys" icon="key">
    Create and rotate API keys for integrations.
  </Card>
  <Card title="OAuth Apps" icon="code">
    Register OAuth apps and manage credentials.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Billing

Manage subscriptions, payment methods, and usage in **Organization Settings** → **Billing**.

## Account Deletion & Export

<Warning>
  Deleting your account permanently removes your videos, comments, and settings.
</Warning>

Use **Settings** → **Account** to export your data or delete your account.
