## Upload & import videos

**Upload from your device:** Click **Upload Video** in the sidebar and drag in MP4, MOV, AVI, or MKV files.

**Import from cloud services:** Click **Import Videos** to connect Google Drive, Zoom, or Google Meet and pull in recordings directly.

## AI-powered insights

Every video automatically gets:

| Feature | Description |
| ------- | ----------- |
| **Transcript** | Searchable text of the full conversation |
| **Summary** | Key points condensed into a few sentences |
| **Chapters** | Auto-generated sections for easy navigation |
| **Action items** | Tasks and follow-ups mentioned in the video |
| **Speakers** | Who said what (speaker diarization) |

## Organize your library

### Channels

Group videos by team or topic (e.g., "Marketing", "Product", "Customer Calls").

### Series

Organize videos in sequence for training, onboarding, or recurring meetings.

### Collections

Curate videos from across channels for specific projects or initiatives.

### Library views

| View | What it shows |
| ---- | ------------- |
| **My Videos** | Videos you uploaded |
| **Watch Later** | Saved for later |
| **History** | Recently watched |
| **Shared with Me** | Videos others shared with you |

## Collaborate with your team

### Share videos

Click **Share** on any video to create a link with:
- **Access level** — View, comment, or download
- **Expiration** — Optional time limit
- **Password** — Optional protection

### Time-stamped comments

Leave feedback at specific moments:
1. Play video to the right timestamp
2. Click the comment icon in the player or transcript
3. Add your note (use @ to mention teammates)

### Activity feed

Track uploads, comments, and changes across your organization from the Home page.

## Search

Press **Cmd/Ctrl + K** anywhere to search across:
- Video titles and descriptions
- Transcript text
- Topics and decisions
- People and mentions

## Integrations

Connect Nuclom to your existing tools:

- **Zoom** — Auto-import meeting recordings
- **Google Meet** — Pull in recordings from Google Drive
- **Slack** — Get notifications and share videos
- **Zapier** — Build custom automations

Set up integrations in **Organization Settings → Integrations**.

## Keyboard shortcuts

| Shortcut | Action |
| -------- | ------ |
| `Cmd/Ctrl + K` | Open search |
| `Space` | Play/pause |
| `←` / `→` | Seek 10 seconds |
| `F` | Fullscreen |
