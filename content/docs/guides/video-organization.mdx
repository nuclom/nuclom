Organized libraries help teams find the right content fast. This guide covers upload options and the core organizational tools in Nuclom.

## Upload & Import

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Upload from your device" icon="upload">
    Drag and drop MP4, MOV, AVI, or MKV files directly into Nuclom.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Import recordings" icon="cloud">
    Bring in videos from Google Drive, Zoom, and Google Meet.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Upload Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Upload">
    Click **Upload Video** in the left navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a file">
    Drag and drop or browse to choose your video.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add details">
    Add a title, description, and optional channel, series, or collection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Upload and let Nuclom process the content.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Import Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Import">
    Click **Import Videos** from the upload area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a source">
    Select Google Drive, Zoom, or Google Meet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick recordings">
    Select the files you want to import.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  During processing, Nuclom generates transcripts, summaries, chapters, and action items.
</Note>

## Library Views

Use library views to keep track of activity and personal queues.

| View | Description |
| ---- | ----------- |
| **My Videos** | Videos you uploaded |
| **Watch Later** | Saved items you plan to watch |
| **History** | Recently watched videos |
| **Shared with Me** | Videos shared by teammates |
| **Trash** | Recently deleted videos |
