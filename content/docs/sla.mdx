## Uptime Commitment

| Tier | Uptime SLA |
| ---- | ---------- |
| **Free** | No SLA |
| **Pro** | 99.9% |
| **Enterprise** | 99.99% |

## Uptime Calculation

```
Uptime % = ((Total Minutes - Downtime Minutes) / Total Minutes) × 100
```

### Scheduled Maintenance

Scheduled maintenance is excluded when:

- Announced at least 72 hours in advance
- Scheduled during low-traffic periods
- Limited to 4 hours per month

## Service Credits

<Note>
  Service credits apply to Pro and Enterprise customers when SLA targets are missed.
</Note>

| Monthly Uptime | Credit (% of monthly bill) |
| -------------- | -------------------------- |
| 99.9% - 99.0% | 10% |
| 99.0% - 95.0% | 25% |
| Below 95.0% | 50% |

### Requesting Credits

<Steps>
  <Step title="Document downtime">
    Note the date, time, and duration of the outage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit request">
    Email sla@nuclom.com within 30 days.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review">
    We’ll verify and apply credits within 10 business days.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Exclusions

The SLA does not apply to:

- Scheduled maintenance
- Beta or preview features
- Customer-caused issues
- Third-party service outages
- Force majeure events
- Free tier accounts
