## Overview

Three-tier pricing for our unified knowledge management platform.

- **No free tier** - all users start with a 14-day trial
- **14-day free trial** (no credit card required)
- **Yearly billing shown as default** (15% savings vs monthly)
- **Single workspace per organization** (no multi-workspace complexity)

- Enterprise features (SSO, audit logs) at accessible price
- Alternative to expensive revenue intelligence platforms

## Trial & Payment Policy

### 14-Day Free Trial

- Full access to all features of chosen plan
- No credit card required to start
- Email reminders at day 7, 12, and 14
- Converts to read-only if no payment after trial

### Payment Terms

- Payment due within 30 days of trial end
- Accepted: Credit card, ACH transfer

### Refund Policy

| Plan | Refund Policy | Details |
|------|---------------|---------|
| **Monthly** | Prorated daily refund | Refund = remaining days Ã— daily rate |
| **Yearly** | Non-refundable | 15% discount reflects commitment |

**Monthly Refund Calculation:**
- Daily rate = Monthly price / 30
- Scale: $34 / 30 = $1.13/day
- Growth: $58 / 30 = $1.93/day
- Pro: $93 / 30 = $3.10/day

### Data Retention

| Scenario | Data Kept | Then Deleted |
|----------|-----------|--------------|
| Trial expires (no payment) | 14 days | Permanently |
| Subscription cancelled | 30 days | Permanently |
| Payment overdue | 30 days (suspended) | After 30 more days |
