## Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Go to [nuclom.com](https://nuclom.com) and choose **Email**, **GitHub**, or **Google**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete your profile">
    Add your name, avatar, and timezone so your team sees the right details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your organization">
    Pick an organization name and slug to create your workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add Your First Video

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Upload a file">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Upload">
        Click **Upload Video** in the left navigation.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose your file">
        Drag and drop a video or browse your computer.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add details">
        Add a title, description, and optional channel or series.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Publish">
        Upload and let Nuclom process the video.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Import recordings">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Import">
        Click **Import Videos** from the upload area.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Connect a source">
        Choose Google Drive, Zoom, or Google Meet.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select recordings">
        Pick files to import and confirm the upload.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  Video processing automatically generates transcripts, summaries, chapters, and action items.
</Note>

## Invite Your Team

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Members settings">
    Go to **Organization Settings** → **Members**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send invitations">
    Add teammates by email and choose their role.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share a video">
    Use the **Share** button to send a secure link or set access levels.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## For Developers (Optional)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clone and install">
    ```bash
    git clone https://github.com/nuclom/nuclom.git
    cd nuclom
    pnpm install
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure environment">
    ```bash
    cp .env.example .env.local
    # Update the values for your environment
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run the app">
    ```bash
    pnpm dev
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  See the [Development Setup](/internal/reference/development-setup) guide for full setup instructions.
</Info>
