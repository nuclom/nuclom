## 1. Create your account

Go to [nuclom.com](https://nuclom.com) and sign up with Email, GitHub, or Google. Create an organization name for your workspace.

## 2. Upload a video

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Upload a file">
    Click **Upload Video** in the sidebar. Drag and drop your video file (MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV).
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Import from cloud">
    Click **Import Videos** and connect Google Drive, Zoom, or Google Meet to pull in recordings.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  AI processing starts automatically. Within minutes you'll have a transcript, summary, chapters, and action items.
</Note>

## 3. Explore insights

Once processing completes:

- **Transcript** — Searchable text of everything said
- **Summary** — Key points in a few sentences
- **Chapters** — Jump to specific sections
- **Action items** — Tasks mentioned in the video

## 4. Share with your team

Click **Share** on any video to generate a link. Choose access level (view, comment, or download) and send it to teammates.

## 5. Search across everything

Use **Cmd/Ctrl + K** to open search. Ask questions like "What did we decide about pricing?" and get answers with timestamps.
