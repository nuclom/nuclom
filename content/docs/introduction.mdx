## Welcome to Nuclom

Nuclom is a video collaboration platform that turns recordings into searchable knowledge. Upload or import videos, organize them into collections, and use AI to generate transcripts, summaries, and decisions your team can act on.

## What You Can Do

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Upload & Import" icon="upload">
    Upload local files or import recordings from Google Drive, Zoom, and Google Meet.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Organize Your Library" icon="folder">
    Use channels, series, and collections to keep workstreams tidy.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Collaborate Faster" icon="messages">
    Share videos, leave time-stamped comments, and mention teammates.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Insights" icon="sparkles">
    Generate transcripts, summaries, chapters, and action items automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Knowledge Hub" icon="lightbulb">
    Explore topics, decisions, gaps, and ask questions across your videos.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Secure & Controlled" icon="shield">
    Role-based access, SSO, audit logs, and org-level settings.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Bring in your videos">
    Upload from your device or import recordings from connected services.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let AI process the content">
    Nuclom generates transcripts, summaries, chapters, and decision insights.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collaborate and share">
    Share videos with teammates, set access levels, and track activity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build a living knowledge base">
    Use Ask, Topics, and Graph to connect information across your library.
  </Step>
</Steps>
