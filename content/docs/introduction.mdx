## What is Nuclom?

Nuclom is a video collaboration platform for teams. Upload meeting recordings, product demos, or training videos and let AI make them searchable, summarized, and actionable.

**In short:** Upload video → AI extracts insights → Team finds answers fast.

## Who it's for

- **Product teams** reviewing user research and demos
- **Sales teams** capturing customer calls and objections
- **Engineering teams** documenting decisions and reviews
- **Any team** drowning in meeting recordings
