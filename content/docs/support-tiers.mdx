## Support Levels

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Free" icon="user">
    Best-effort email support.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pro" icon="star">
    24-hour response time with priority queue.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Enterprise" icon="building">
    Priority support with phone and dedicated Slack access.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Comparison

| Feature | Free | Pro | Enterprise |
| ------- | ---- | --- | ---------- |
| **Response Time** | Best effort | 24 hours | 4 hours |
| **Email Support** | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Priority Queue** | No | Yes | Yes |
| **Phone Support** | No | No | Yes |
| **Dedicated Slack** | No | No | Yes |
| **Account Manager** | No | No | Yes |
| **SLA Guarantee** | No | Yes | Yes |

## Response Times

| Priority | Free | Pro | Enterprise |
| -------- | ---- | --- | ---------- |
| **Critical** | 48 hours | 8 hours | 1 hour |
| **High** | 72 hours | 24 hours | 4 hours |
| **Medium** | 5 days | 48 hours | 8 hours |
| **Low** | Best effort | 72 hours | 24 hours |

## How to Get Support

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the docs">
    Review our [troubleshooting guide](/guides/troubleshooting).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Email support">
    Send details to support@nuclom.com.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enterprise escalation">
    Use your dedicated Slack channel or phone line for urgent issues.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to Include

- Account email and organization name
- Description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce
- Screenshots or error messages
- Browser and device information
