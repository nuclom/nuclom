# Nuclom

> All Your Knowledge, One Intelligent Hub

Nuclom is a unified knowledge platform that aggregates content from Slack, Notion, GitHub, and videos. AI-powered processing surfaces decisions, tracks expertise, and connects ideas across your entire organization.

## Features

- **Ask AI** — Chat with your knowledge base and get instant answers with sources
- **Knowledge Feed** — Unified activity feed across all your connected sources
- **Topic Explorer** — Auto-clustered topics showing what your organization discusses
- **Decision Tracking** — Never lose a decision again with full context and rationale
- **Slack Integration** — Sync conversations, threads, and announcements
- **Notion Integration** — Connect wikis, documentation, and project notes
- **GitHub Integration** — Index PRs, issues, discussions, and wikis
- **Video & Meetings** — AI transcription extracts insights from recordings
- **Knowledge Graph** — Discover relationships between content across all sources
- **Expertise Mapping** — Know who to ask based on contribution history
- **Unified Search** — Semantic search across all your knowledge sources
- **Enterprise Security** — SSO/SAML, role-based access, and audit logs
