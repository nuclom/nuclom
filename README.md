# Nuclom

> Video collaboration for modern teams

Nuclom is a video collaboration platform that helps teams upload, organize, and collaborate on video content with AI-powered insights.

## Features

- **Video Management** — Upload, organize, and share videos with your team
- **AI-Powered Insights** — Automatic summaries, transcriptions, and action item extraction
- **Time-Stamped Comments** — Leave feedback at specific moments in videos
- **Team Collaboration** — Multi-organization support with role-based access control
- **Integrations** — Connect with Zoom, Google Drive, and more
- **Smart Search** — Semantic search across video content and transcripts

## Getting Started

Visit [nuclom.com](https://nuclom.com) to create your account and start collaborating.
